Office Hours

Just like professors, Tom holds weekly office hours. These are an excellent time for quick questions, or to workin on something that does not fit within the context of a 1:1.

1:1s

As part of living in a Residential Learning Community at San Diego State, all residents are required to have at least three 1:1 meetings with their academic mentor during the first semester to ensure that they are successful academically.

Schedule an Appointment

STAR Center

The STAR Centers are an academic resource center for all residence hall students. The centers help create a positive, academically-orientated environment to help residence hall students achieve academic success.

Places

Supplemental Instruction

Peer-Lead, Non Remedial, Group Learning. It has been shown to raise student grades by as much as a whole letter grade! Available for Math 150, 151, 254; STAT 119; CHEM 100, 200, 232; PSY 101.

Check Your Blackboard Course!

Math Learning Center

Need help with MATH 141, 150, 151, 252, Stat 119 or 250, or Business Calc? The MLC has tutors for you. This is also a great place to meet up with other students from your Math/Stats classes to work on homework/assignments, since there will often be somebody with experience in that area in the MLC.

Visit their Website

Writing Center

Need someone to read over your essay or outline? The Writing Center has you covered. They also offer a wide array of workshops throughout the semester, like WPA Prep and other writing related topics.

Visit their Website

Department Tutoring Offices

Many departments have support for courses within their department. This can come in the forms of Help Rooms, like is the case for Chemistry, or a Computer Lab, like is the case for Computer Science and many departments within the College of Engineering.

Check Your Blackboard Course or your Department's Website!
